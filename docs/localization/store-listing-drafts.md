标题：龙卷突袭

完整描述：

龙卷突袭是一款轻松上手的 3D 休闲小游戏。拖动屏幕控制龙卷风，在城市街区中吸收小汽车、公交车、房屋和特殊建筑。先从小物件开始成长，再挑战发电厂、警察局、消防局等大型目标。

每一局都是一场快速城市清扫：观察体量、选择路线、抓住时间，在倒计时结束前变成最大的龙卷风。

## en

Title: Tornado Strike

Short description: Steer a growing tornado, swallow the city, and clear timed challenges.

Full description:

Tornado Strike is a quick 3D casual game about growing from a tiny storm into a city-sized tornado. Drag to steer, absorb cars, buses, houses, and special buildings, then grow big enough to take down power plants, police stations, and fire stations.

Plan your route, pick the right targets, and keep growing before time runs out.
